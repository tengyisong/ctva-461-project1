TIPS

1.It's better to go early than to go late

The EDC of Las Vegas is typically in May, with three consecutive days. From sunset to sunrise, it starts at 7.30pm each night and ends around 5am the next day. As far as physical strength is concerned, it is impossible to participate from the beginning to end. Go early and leave early to prevent traffic jams. Traffic jams are very scary. We hire a car this time. We usually start at around 9pm and arrive around 1am.

2.Water bag

The bottled water inside is very expensive, about 5-6 bottles. There is a special free water station in the venue. The advantages of water bags, in addition to a lot of water, can also be used as a small bag to place some change and bank CARDS, very practical.

3.Choose your vehicle wisely and remember your parking space

There will be shuttle buses to the scene from the city, or uber. The advantage is that it's easy to get there without getting stuck in traffic. The disadvantage is just very difficult when you come back. Both uber and shuttle are required to wait. It is a horrible thing to wait for the bus when you are very tired. If you drive your own car, in addition to traffic jams, more important is probably in the parking lot, can not find the car!! There could be hundreds of thousands of cars flooding into the parking lot at the same time, and at night, it's important to remember your parking location, and it's best to plug a balloon into the car so you can see it from afar.
